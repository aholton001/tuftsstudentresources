How long is my fridge rental for?

Your rental is for the school year, it will be delivered the week before school starts and will be picked up during finals period of spring semester.

I broke my microwave glass plate, what should I do?

You will be charged $20, please fill out the repair form and mention that you broke your microwave plate. If you don't do this immediately during the school year you will still be charged $20 at pick up.

Can I request that my Microwave be detached?

No. The microwave of the microfridge is only allowed by Residential life because of the safe-plug technology that prevents dangerous electrical failures from taking place.
